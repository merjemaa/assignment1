MOST USED PHONE IN 2024

IPHONE 14

The iPhone 14 features a 6.1-inch OLED display protected by ceramic glass.
It offers a standard 60Hz refresh rate, which was unexpected as only the Pro models in the iPhone 14 series have higher refresh rates.
The phone is constructed with glass and aluminum frames and boasts an IP68 rating for water and dust resistance.
The design remains consistent with previous iPhone models.
It is equipped with the A15 Bionic chipset, the same as the iPhone 13.
It features 6GB of RAM and up to 512GB of storage.
The rear camera setup includes dual 12MP cameras, while the front-facing camera is also 12MP.
The 3279mAh battery is capable of lasting a full day on a single charge.

iPhone 14 Quick Specs
Display Size: 6.1 inch (1170×2532)
Display Type: Super Retina XDR OLED
RAM and Storage: 6GB RAM & 128, 256, or 512GB
Operating System: IOS 16
Chipset: A15 Bionic
Camera: 12MP + 12MP Selfie: 12MP
Battery: 3279mAh
